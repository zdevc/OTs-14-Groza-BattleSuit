## 📦 Installation

### Method 1: Steam Workshop (Recommended)

1. Visit [Workshop Page](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3694560137)
2. Click **"Subscribe"**
3. Enable mod in-game

### Method 2: Manual Install

> ⚠️ Use this method if character shows as white box (missing textures).

1. [Download Mod File](https://github.com/zdevc/OTs-14-Groza-BattleSuit/releases/download/mod/OTs-14.Groza.BattleSuit.zip)
2. Extract to: `C:\Users\[Username]\AppData\Roaming\JaggedAlliance3\Mods`
3. Enable in-game

> 💡 `AppData` is a hidden folder. Enable "Show hidden files" in folder options.

## 📖 Background Story

OTs-14, codenamed **"Groza"**, is the adjutant of the Elmo and captain of the Lightning Squad. A former Griffin elite, she was remotely formatted for questioning orders, lost her memories, and was found by the Commander in the Yellow Zone wasteland — given a new life aboard the Elmo.

For ten years, Groza has been the Commander's most trusted right hand — expert in tactical command, Doll coordination, and mission planning. Usually mature and steady, she has a cute side: to save money, she never installed a taste module, so her cooking is... let's just say it has a unique "Groza special flavor."

Recently, mysterious infections emerged in Grand Chien. Groza was dispatched to lead a squad on reconnaissance. For her, this is not just a mission — it's a test of her independent command capabilities.

**Lightning Squad Members**: Kolph, Nemesiss, Krolik, Vipre
